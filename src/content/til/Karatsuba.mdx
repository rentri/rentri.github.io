---
title: Computers multiply big numbers fast by reducing multiplication
date: 01-16-2026
slug: katsurba
---

The [Karatsuba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karatsuba_algorithm) algorithm reduces the number of multiplications needed for two n-digit numbers that 
require `k` multiplication steps by substituting them with `k-1` multiplications plus an addition instead 
of `k` multiplications, as multiplication is asymptotically more expensive than addition.
